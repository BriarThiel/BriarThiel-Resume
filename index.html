Hi there! My name is Briar Thiel

I am a Global Studies Major. My favorite pastimes are fencing at the UCR fencing club as well as skateboarding, and producing music. I enjoy spending time with friends and eating out. In the future I want to be a traveling journalist reporting on international conflicts in the world. Specifically I want to be a war-zone reporter. I am currently attending UCR as an out-of-state student. I am originally from Atlanta, Georgia. I love traveling but have only been out of the country once. I went to Paris, France over the summer of 2022 and enjoyed it a lot. I spoke some French at the time so it luckily made navigating the public transit fairly easy for my family. I had fun growing accustomed to the culture as it is very different from culture in America. The food was amazing too.

Experiencing other cultures first-hand is very important to me as I grew up in a small town surrounded by a very dull and melancholic culture. Meeting new people excites me and I hope I can translate that towards my career in the future. I hope to learn new languages as well. I have taken French in middle school, and Latin in high school, which I took very seriously by participating in the club it was associated with. I want to get more involved in learning Swedish as I took it on Duolingo for a while but couldn't keep up and eventually dropped it entirely. Scandanavia is a very cool region to me and I definitely want to look into studying abroad in Copenhagen or Stockholm. Me and a friend were looking into UCR's resources for their abroad program and I think it's something I want to pursue. I have studied their culture and admire it even more then that of America's. One day I want to visit all of the continents in the world and do some sort of reporting on each one. There are a lot of issues in our world and I think it is important to educate people on said issues to bring more attention to those issues that ravage societies and communities across the globe.
